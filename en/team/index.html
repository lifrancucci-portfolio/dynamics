Our Team

Marcos Orteu

Associate Director

Marcos specializes in strategy, digital economics and regulation/antitrust. He has experience working in telco, digital, media, agribusiness and carbon capture projects, among others, for leading companies, startups and regulators.

He is a professor at Torcuato Di Tella University (UTDT), Buenos Aires Technology Institute (ITBA) and UCEMAx where he teaches a variety of courses based on strategy/IO, digital platforms and applied microeconomics.

He holds a Master's in Economics from University of San Andrés, and a LLM in Law and Economics from Torcuato Di Tella University.
